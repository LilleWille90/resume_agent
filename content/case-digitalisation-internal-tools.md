# Case: Digitalising internal workflows at Epidemic Sound

## Context
Fast-growing organisations often end up with scattered processes, tools, and knowledge, which slows teams down.

## Problem
Internal workflows didn’t scale well with growth, creating friction and inefficiency across departments.

## Approach
- Partnered closely with stakeholders to map needs and prioritise improvements
- Implemented software/process changes and rolled out solutions iteratively
- Managed software budget and vendor negotiations to enable scalable tooling
- Planned and executed projects using OKRs on yearly/quarterly cadence

## Result (qualitative)
Improved internal workflows through software, automation, and new tools, enabling the organisation to operate more effectively as it scaled.

## Learnings
- Internal “products” need the same discovery/rollout discipline as external ones
- Strong stakeholder alignment + clear prioritisation is everything
